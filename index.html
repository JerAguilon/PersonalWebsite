About me

Currently I'm a second year in Georgia Tech's Computer Science program, set to graduate May 2018. I self-taught myself the gateway drug we call Java in high school and have been hooked since.

If I had to pick my favorite personal project, it's hands down my Icon-Based Asthma Action Plan, which I did for research out in Tech Square. As somebody who was constantly troubled by the disease, I know the struggles of managing the condition, even with parents who were highly literate. I find value in knowing that my work could measurably improve the lives of others around me.

An ongoing collaborative project that aims to provide a means for people to crowd source clean water to the 783 million people worldwide who do not have access to potable water.
